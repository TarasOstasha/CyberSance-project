Strategic Planning

We offer top-of-the-line Strategic Planning based on Predictive Power of Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI).

In order to unleash the predictive power of Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI), organizations must integrate data collection, analysis, and actionable insights with advanced technologies and strategic approaches. Predictive CTI enables businesses to anticipate attacks, mitigate risks, and develop proactive defenses. Here is what we do to effectively harness the predictive capabilities of CTI:

Continuously Build a Comprehensive Data Collection Framework

A robust data collection framework is the foundation for predictive CTI.

Analyze Adversary TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures)

Studying adversary behavior enhances predictive capabilities by uncovering likely attack patterns.

Employ Advanced Machine Learning (ML) Models

AI and ML amplify CTI by automating data analysis and identifying patterns that human analysts might miss. Key actions include developing predictive models, detecting anomalies and correlating threat indicators.

Focus on Vulnerability Prioritization

Proactively addressing vulnerabilities likely to be exploited ensures readiness against impending threats. The best way to achieve this is by using predictive vulnerability scoring and monitoring exploit chatter.

Harness Real-Time Intelligence and Alerts

Timely information enables swift defensive actions. Key steps to achieve this include setting up alerts for emerging threats or specific indicators, as well as monitoring geopolitical trends. It is crucial to correlate cyber threats with global political and economic events to predict nation-state actions.

Create Actionable Intelligence

Predictive power is maximized when insights are actionable and aligned with business goals. The best way to accomplish this is by aligning intelligence with risk management, tailoring the insights to the organization’s assets, sectors, and threat models. Another step is by delivering intelligence reports with context, such as likely attack methods, expected timelines, and recommendations.

Foster Collaboration and Intelligence Sharing

Collaborative efforts enhance predictive capabilities by pooling intelligence from multiple sources. Participation in Threat Sharing Communities in this case is crucial, to share information with industry peers to organize collective defense against any active campaigns.

Visualization and Reporting

Visualizing intelligence makes complex data more accessible for decision-making. We emphasize the importance of creating real-time visualizations of threat trends and predictions - to communicate the insights clearly.

Continuously Evaluate and Evolve

Cyber threats evolve rapidly, and CTI strategies must adapt to stay effective. In order to achieve that there are two important processes to consider and implement; first, it is crucial to review prediction accuracy by comparing predicted threats to actual incidents and refining models accordingly. And second, to incorporate feedback loops by using feedback from security teams to improve intelligence processes. By allowing us to implement these strategies for them, organizations can transition from reactive defenses to proactive, predictive security, gaining a decisive advantage in the ever-evolving threat landscape.
